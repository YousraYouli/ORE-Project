about us

As a team , we are committed to providing an exceptional programming experience. We prioritize user satisfaction, simplicity, and creativity.
At Ore , we provide you the user , with a user-friendly and math oriented interpreted language that can help you build your programs and projects . And through our site’s interface you’ll have the chance to test small programs and have an insight on the basic operations you can realize with the language , without having to install it first.
As an extra bonus point , you’ll be working with a convenient and simple syntax with little to no restrictions which makes it the perfect choice for you as a begginer.
As a user of Ore, you can expect a helpful and responsive support team to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter. We strive to provide the best possible experience for our users, and we are always looking for ways to improve. Don't hesitate to reach out to us if you need help or have any suggestions for how we can make Ore even better.
